Integrated Programme (Direct School Admission)

Direct School Admission into 6-year IP Programme for Academic Year 2021 – DSA-Sec Exercise 2020

The Direct School Admission-Sec (DSA-Sec) Exercise is conducted annually. We invite all interested 2020 Primary 6 students to apply for National Junior College’s 6-year Integrated Programme via the DSA-Sec Exercise.

Parents and students are encouraged to choose schools wisely based on the student’s aptitudes and strengths, bearing in mind the schools’ academic and non-academic requirements, and the programmes available to develop their potential and talents.

Students who are successfully admitted to National Junior College via DSA-Sec are expected to honour their commitment to the school for the duration of the six-year Integrated Programme, and participate in the activities related to the talent they are selected for throughout Junior High (i.e. Years 1 – 4).

Application

Applications for the DSA-Sec are free-of-charge and must be submitted through the online DSA-Sec Portal. Applications open on 12 May 2020 (Tue), 9 am and close on 5 June 2020 (Fri), 3 pm.

Please refer to the MOE website: www.moe.gov.sg/dsa-sec for more details.

Selection Process

Applicants will be shortlisted and selected based on the following general criteria:

  1. Demonstrate passion for and commitment to the area of interest;
  2. Exemplify positive character traits, including resilience, motivation and integrity;
  3. Display a commitment to contribute to the school, through the area of interest as well as other opportunities to serve; and
  4. Possess good academic potential to thrive in the Integrated Programme.

In addition, each talent area has specific criteria. Please refer to this page for the details.

All shortlisted candidates will be notified via email by 17 July 2020 to attend an e-interview via video conferencing with the Principal, Vice-Principals, HODs and/or the CCA teachers. The video conference may also include an audition / trial. More details on the performance task will be provided in the email notification. In light of the Covid-19 situation, we will not be conducting any in-person interviews, auditions or trials.

Applicants must attend the e-interview from their current primary school.

The e-interviews will take place between 1 July and 14 September 2020.

Offer of Places

Applicants who are shortlisted for interviews will be notified via email of the results of the DSA-Sec application by 14 September 2020. Successful applicants will be offered a place in National Junior College’s 6-year Integrated Programme for the academic year 2021.
